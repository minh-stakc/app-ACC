Approach to automatic agriculture

Automatic agriculture, also known as smart farming, utilizes advanced technologies like IoT and data analytics to optimize crop production and resource management. Through real-time monitoring of environmental factors and data-driven insights, farmers can make informed decisions about irrigation, fertilization, and pest control. This approach maximizes efficiency, minimizes resource wastage, and contributes to sustainable and high-yield agricultural practices.
